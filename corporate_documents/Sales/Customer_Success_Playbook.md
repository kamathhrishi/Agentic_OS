# Customer Success Playbook

## Overview
Comprehensive guide for ensuring customer success, retention, and growth.

## Customer Lifecycle

### Onboarding (Days 0-90)
**Goal:** Rapid time to value

**Activities:**
- Kickoff meeting within 48 hours
- Technical setup and configuration
- User training sessions
- Success criteria definition
- Regular check-ins (weekly)

**Success Metrics:**
- Time to first value: <30 days
- User adoption: 60% active users
- Training completion: 100%
- Initial goals achieved: 80%

### Adoption (Days 90-180)
**Goal:** Deep product usage

**Activities:**
- Advanced feature training
- Use case expansion
- Best practice sharing
- Community engagement
- Quarterly business reviews

**Success Metrics:**
- Feature adoption: 70% of key features
- Usage growth: 25% month-over-month
- Support tickets: <5 per month
- Satisfaction score: 4.0+

### Growth (Days 180-365)
**Goal:** Expansion and advocacy

**Activities:**
- Expansion opportunities identification
- ROI demonstration
- Reference customer development
- Upsell/cross-sell conversations
- Annual business review

**Success Metrics:**
- Expansion revenue: 20%+
- Net Revenue Retention: 110%+
- Reference customer: Yes
- Renewal probability: 90%+

### Renewal (Year 1+)
**Goal:** Successful renewal and expansion

**Activities:**
- Renewal planning (90 days prior)
- Value realization review
- Contract negotiation
- Expansion planning
- Strategic account planning

**Success Metrics:**
- Renewal rate: 95%+
- Expansion rate: 30%+
- Customer Lifetime Value: Growing
- Advocacy: NPS 50+

## Customer Health Scoring

### Health Indicators
- **Product Usage:** Feature adoption, login frequency
- **Support:** Ticket volume, resolution time
- **Engagement:** Meeting attendance, training participation
- **Business Outcomes:** Goal achievement, ROI
- **Relationship:** Stakeholder engagement, feedback

### Health Tiers
- **Green (Healthy):** 80-100 points
  - On track for success
  - Expansion opportunities
  - Reference potential

- **Yellow (At Risk):** 50-79 points
  - Requires attention
  - Intervention needed
  - Risk mitigation plan

- **Red (Critical):** 0-49 points
  - High churn risk
  - Immediate action required
  - Executive engagement needed

## Engagement Strategies

### High-Touch Customers (Enterprise)
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager
- Weekly check-ins
- Quarterly business reviews
- Executive sponsorships
- Custom success plans

### Mid-Touch Customers (Mid-Market)
- Assigned Customer Success Manager
- Bi-weekly check-ins
- Quarterly business reviews
- Standard success plans
- Community access

### Low-Touch Customers (SMB)
- Shared Customer Success resources
- Monthly communications
- Self-service resources
- Community support
- Automated check-ins

## Expansion Strategies

### User Expansion
- Identify additional departments
- Demonstrate value to new teams
- Provide use cases
- Training support

### Feature Expansion
- Identify feature gaps
- Demonstrate ROI of premium features
- Trial periods
- Migration support

### Product Expansion
- Cross-sell complementary products
- Integration opportunities
- Bundled offerings
- Referral incentives

## Risk Mitigation

### Early Warning Signs
- Declining usage
- Support ticket spikes
- Missed meetings
- Negative feedback
- Budget constraints

### Intervention Process
1. Identify risk indicators
2. Assess severity
3. Develop action plan
4. Engage stakeholders
5. Execute recovery plan
6. Monitor progress

- Expansion Revenue
