# Sales Process Documentation

## Sales Methodology
Our sales process follows a structured approach to identify, qualify, develop, and close opportunities.

## Sales Stages

### 1. Prospecting
**Goal:** Identify potential customers
**Activities:**
- Market research
- Lead generation
- Outreach campaigns
- Event participation
- Referral programs

**Success Criteria:**
- Quality lead identification
- Contact information obtained
- Initial interest confirmed

### 2. Qualification
**Goal:** Determine fit and interest
**Activities:**
- Initial discovery call
- Needs assessment
- Budget qualification
- Timeline discussion
- Stakeholder identification

**BANT Qualification:**
- **Budget:** Approved budget available
- **Authority:** Decision maker engaged
- **Need:** Clear business need
- **Timeline:** Defined timeline

**Success Criteria:**
- Qualified opportunity
- Next steps agreed
- Meeting scheduled

### 3. Discovery
**Goal:** Understand requirements deeply
**Activities:**
- Detailed needs analysis
- Technical requirements gathering
- Stakeholder meetings
- Process mapping
- Pain point identification

**Deliverables:**
- Discovery summary document
- Requirements document
- Stakeholder map

**Success Criteria:**
- Complete requirements understanding
- All stakeholders identified
- Business case validated

### 4. Solution Design
**Goal:** Develop tailored solution
**Activities:**
- Solution architecture design
- Customization requirements
- Integration planning
- Implementation approach
- ROI analysis

**Deliverables:**
- Solution proposal
- Technical architecture
- Implementation plan
- Pricing proposal

**Success Criteria:**
- Solution aligned with needs
- Technical feasibility confirmed
- Pricing provided

### 5. Proposal
**Goal:** Present formal proposal
**Activities:**
- Proposal presentation
- Demo/trial setup
- Reference customer calls
- Q&A sessions
- Negotiation

**Deliverables:**
- Formal proposal document
- Contract terms
- SLA documentation

**Success Criteria:**
- Proposal accepted
- Terms agreed
- Ready for contract

### 6. Negotiation
**Goal:** Finalize agreement
**Activities:**
- Contract negotiation
- Legal review
- Terms finalization
- Pricing discussion
- SLA agreement

**Success Criteria:**
- Contract executed
- Terms finalized
- Project kickoff scheduled

### 7. Closed Won
**Goal:** Successful deal closure
**Activities:**
- Contract execution
- Handoff to customer success
- Kickoff meeting
- Implementation planning

**Success Criteria:**
- Contract signed
- Payment received
- Implementation started

## Sales Roles

### Account Executive
- Primary responsibility: New customer acquisition
- Quota: $2M annually
- Focus: Mid-market and enterprise

### Business Development Rep
- Primary responsibility: Lead generation and qualification
- Quota: 20 qualified opportunities/month
- Focus: Outbound prospecting

### Sales Engineer
- Primary responsibility: Technical sales support
- Focus: Product demos, technical evaluation
