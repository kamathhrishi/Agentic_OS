# Vendor Management Policy

## Purpose
Establish guidelines for selecting, managing, and evaluating vendors to ensure quality service delivery and risk mitigation.

## Vendor Selection Process

### 1. Requirements Definition
- Business need identification
- Technical requirements
- Budget constraints
- Timeline requirements

### 2. Vendor Research
- Market research
- Vendor identification (minimum 3 vendors)
- Initial qualification
- Capability assessment

### 3. Request for Proposal (RFP)
- RFP document preparation
- Distribution to qualified vendors
- Response deadline: 2 weeks
- Q&A session

### 4. Evaluation
- Scoring matrix: Cost (30%), Capability (40%), References (15%), Terms (15%)
- Technical evaluation
- Reference checks (minimum 3)
- Financial stability review

### 5. Selection
- Evaluation committee review
- Recommendation to management
- Approval process
- Vendor selection

## Vendor Onboarding
- Contract execution
- Vendor setup in system
- Access provisioning
- Kickoff meeting
- Performance expectations communicated

## Vendor Management

### Ongoing Relationship
- Regular communication
- Quarterly business reviews
- Performance monitoring
- Issue resolution

### Performance Metrics
- Service level adherence
- Quality of deliverables
- Response times
- Cost performance
- Innovation contributions

### Contract Management
- Terms and conditions compliance
- Renewal process (90 days prior)
- Amendment procedures
- Termination procedures

## Vendor Categories

### Strategic Partners
- Long-term relationships
- Joint product development
- Co-marketing activities
- Quarterly executive reviews

### Preferred Vendors
- Regular business relationship
- Standard terms and pricing
- Quarterly reviews

### Approved Vendors
- Occasional use
- Project-based engagement
- Annual reviews

## Risk Management
- Vendor financial stability
- Security and compliance
- Business continuity
- Dependency assessment
- Backup vendor identification

## Vendor Offboarding
- Termination notice
- Transition planning
- Knowledge transfer
- Final deliverables
- Access revocation
- Final payment

## Responsibilities
- **Procurement:** Vendor selection and contracts
- **Department Heads:** Requirements and evaluation
- **Finance:** Budget approval and payments
- **Legal:** Contract review and terms
- **Operations:** Ongoing management
