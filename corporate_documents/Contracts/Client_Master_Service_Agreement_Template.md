# Master Service Agreement Template

## Standard Terms and Conditions

### 1. Services
Provider agrees to deliver services as specified in individual Statements of Work (SOWs).

### 2. Payment Terms
- Net 30 payment terms
- Late fees: 1.5% per month
- Disputes must be raised within 30 days

### 3. Intellectual Property
- Client owns all deliverables
- Provider retains rights to pre-existing IP
- Joint development requires separate agreement

### 4. Warranties
- Services will be performed with professional competence
- Compliance with applicable laws and regulations
- Data security and privacy standards

### 5. Limitation of Liability
- Maximum liability: 12 months of fees
- Exclusions: indirect damages, lost profits

### 6. Confidentiality
- Mutual NDA terms apply
- Duration: 3 years post-termination

### 7. Term and Termination
- Term as specified in SOW
- Termination for cause: 30 days notice
- Termination for convenience: 60 days notice
