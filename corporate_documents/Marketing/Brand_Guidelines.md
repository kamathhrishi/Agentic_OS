# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity
Our brand represents innovation, reliability, and customer-centricity.

## Logo Usage
- Primary logo: Full color on light backgrounds
- Secondary logo: Monochrome version
- Minimum size: 100px width
- Clear space: 1x logo height on all sides
- Never distort or modify logo

## Color Palette
### Primary Colors
- **Primary Blue:** #0066CC (RGB: 0, 102, 204)
- **Secondary Blue:** #003366 (RGB: 0, 51, 102)
- **Accent Orange:** #FF6600 (RGB: 255, 102, 0)

### Neutral Colors
- **Dark Gray:** #333333
- **Medium Gray:** #666666
- **Light Gray:** #CCCCCC
- **White:** #FFFFFF

## Typography
### Headings
- Font: Inter Bold
- Sizes: 32px, 24px, 20px, 18px
- Color: Primary Blue or Dark Gray

### Body Text
- Font: Inter Regular
- Size: 16px (desktop), 14px (mobile)
- Line Height: 1.5
- Color: Dark Gray

## Tone of Voice
- **Professional yet approachable**
- Clear and concise
- Solution-oriented
- Customer-focused
- Confident but not arrogant

## Imagery Style
- High-quality professional photography
- Diverse and inclusive representation
- Modern, clean aesthetic
- Technology-forward visuals
- Authentic workplace scenes

## Messaging Framework
- **Headline:** Clear value proposition
- **Subheadline:** Supporting details
- **Body:** Benefits and features
- **CTA:** Action-oriented language

## Application Examples
- Website design
- Marketing materials
- Presentations
- Sales collateral
- Social media

## Do's and Don'ts
### Do's
- Use approved colors and fonts
- Maintain consistent spacing
- Follow accessibility guidelines
- Ensure high-quality images

### Don'ts
- Don't use unapproved fonts
- Don't modify logo
- Don't use low-resolution images
- Don't ignore brand colors
